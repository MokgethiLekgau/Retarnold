About


Primary schools are the first kind of school that children go to upon their enrollment in the educational system. Since it’s often a student’s first encounter with formal learning, it’s an important step in their education.

Dipakapakeng Primary School is a public School in the Tafelkop Community at Dipakapakeng Section.

The school was found in 1979 , it is really a good asset for the community because it helps the parents with raising the kids and keeping them away from the streets.

Our Mission Is to promote best interest of the school and to ensure its development by means of quality education and train learners to adjust to the global community needs.

Primary schools are educational institutions designed to teach basic education and prepare students for the more advanced secondary level12. Primary schooling follows preschool and precedes secondary schooling2. The International Standard Classification of Education considers primary education as a single phase where programmes are typically designed to provide fundamental skills in reading, writing, and mathematics and to establish a solid foundation for learning2. Primary school lays the foundation for learning and should help kids feel great about how they wonder, explore, play, and ask questions
